eZ\Publish\Core\MVC\Symfony\View\ContentViewInterface::setTemplateIdentifier (   $templateIdentifier)

Sets $templateIdentifier to the content view. Can be either a valid template identifier such as "MyBundle:subfolder:my_template.html.twig" or a closure. In the case of a closure, it will receive an array of parameters as an argument and must return the result to display.

Must throw a exception if $templateIdentifier is invalid.
